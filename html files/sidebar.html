Alphabet

In English language, there are 26 letters. Out of which 5 are vowels(a,e,i,o,u) and remaining ones are consonants.

Numbers(1-100)

Numbers 1 to 100 are the basic building blocks of counting. They help us understand quantity and order. From simple 1 to complex 100, they're crucial for learning math.

Days of the Week

Weekdays: Monday is the start, back to work or school. Tuesday is easier, Wednesday is the middle, perfect for tasks. Thursday brings excitement for the weekend, Friday is freedom day! Weekends: Saturday is for fun and hobbies, Sunday is for errands, chores, and getting ready for the new week.
