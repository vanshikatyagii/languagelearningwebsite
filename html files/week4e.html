Sentences

Sentences are complete thoughts! They use nouns, verbs, and other words to express ideas. Think of them like mini-stories with a subject (who/what) and a predicate (what they do/are).

Types of Sentences

Declarative: Tell something (e.g., "She ate lunch.") Interrogative: Ask a question (e.g., "Where are you going?") Exclamatory: Express strong emotion (e.g., "Wow, that's amazing!") Imperative: Give a command (e.g., "Close the door.)

Sentence Formation

Sentences have a basic structure: Subject + Verb.Subject: Who/what does the action (e.g., "The dog") Verb: The action itself (e.g., "runs") You can add details with adjectives, adverbs, and objects.
